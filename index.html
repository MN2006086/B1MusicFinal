French B1 Music
Songs

Ne me quitte pas by Jacques Brel, 1959

An emotional, metaphorical and poetic French song, Brel expresses desperation and love in a truly deep, and beautoful manner.
Genre: Chanson française, Pop
Skills Improved:
Speaking, Writing, Grammar, Cultural Awareness



Le vent nous portera by Noir Désir, 2001

A legendary French rock song, the deeply introspective and poetic song symbolises time, change and fate with its abstract lyrics. You may recognize it, it being used in films and commercials.
Genre: Alternative Rock, Chanson française, Folk
Skills Improved:
Speaking, Writing, Grammar



La bohème by Charles Aznavour, 1965

A timeless classic recounting the nostalgic story of an artist in Montmarte, Paris' cultural center. It evokes the artistic culture of Paris in an emotional, visual manner, becoming a classic masterpiece in descriptvie storytelling.
Genre: Chanson française
Skills Improved:
Speaking, Writing, Grammar, Cultural Awareness
